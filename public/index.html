Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

Sara brings the soul of Ethiopian hospitality to your table—berbere and niter kibbeh, injera as plate and utensil, and the ritual of eating together.

Our dishes honor Orthodox fasting traditions with rich vegan options, and every meal can end with our traditional coffee ceremony, roasted and brewed in the Ethiopian way.
